About

The Galvanize Foundation, a 501(c)(3), exists to make opportunities in technology available to all those with aptitude, drive and determination, not just those who went to the “right school” and got the "right degree."

We award scholarships to help pay for skills training needed to enter the technology workforce. Specifically, we award scholarships to admitted Galvanize students in immersive web development and data science courses.

What We Fund

We award scholarships to underrepresented populations in technology, including women, minorities, veterans, and persons who identify as LGBT. We also assess financial need, and value diverse life experience and educational backgrounds.

Our goal is to make immersive tech training more financially accessible for all qualified students.

Data on Us

We support programs that create measurable, transformative individual outcomes. That’s why we invest in student scholarships at Galvanize, an outcomes-based technology training school.

Galvanize student placement data speaks for itself - Graduates have a proven track record in successfully transitioning from classroom to workforce.
